Real-World Case Study: The WannaCry Ransomware Attack

In 2017, the WannaCry ransomware attack spread across 150 countries, affecting hospitals, businesses, and government institutions. The attack exploited a vulnerability in Windows operating systems, encrypting user data and demanding ransom payments in Bitcoin.

Lessons Learned:

  • Keep software and operating systems updated to patch vulnerabilities.
  • Implement robust backup and recovery strategies.
  • Educate employees on phishing and ransomware prevention.
